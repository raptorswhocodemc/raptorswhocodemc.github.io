Welcome to RWC.

We love to code.

As the Rockville campus computer science club and We aim to provide our members with a friendly space to learn and have more hands-on experiences in computer science. Being a member of RWC it is totally a non pressure experience: you are not required to be a computer-related major or to have exceptional coding skill. Our goal is just to help our members build their portfolios by participating in little group projects, making social connections, holding tech-related talks, attending hackathons (virtually), and providing a positive, friendly environment for everyone to learn.

Something new about our club is that in addition to being an MC club, we are also an ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) student chapter and as an effect, we benefit to access to special guest speakers and the possibilities to attend some seminars. We will get you to know more about our ACM affiliation throughout the semester and its benefits.
